DEVELOPMENT REPORT

At Young Life Greater New York City, we're re-imagining what sustainable ministry looks like in one of the most diverse cities in the world. Like the Freedom Tower, we stand as a symbol of enduring hope—building an equitable funding model that empowers diverse, called leaders to serve young people long-term. We're not just raising money—we’re raising up people, purpose, and possibility. Through our approach to fundraising, we Inform, Inspire, Involve, and Increase—so every borough thrives, and Young Life becomes a blueprint for justice-driven ministry across the nation.

MINISTRY UPDATE

Dear Friends, Thank you for being a vital part of the Young Life Greater New York City family. Your generosity has fueled relationships, built trust, and helped us walk alongside young people in some of the most vulnerable communities in our region. As we press forward to create lasting sustainability, we want to take a bold step toward transparency, collaboration, and shared vision.

Starting this quarter, you will receive a Quarterly Development Report—a heartfelt update that includes a real story of impact, measurable progress, clear next steps, and prayer requests. It’s more than just data; it’s a glimpse into the holy work we are doing together.

This work is urgent. Just last month, 13-year-old Gustavo—like so many teens searching for identity and belonging—chose to subway surf on his morning commute. He climbed between cars and onto the roof of the train. Tragically, he lost his footing and fell. Today, he’s in critical condition. Gustavo’s story is heartbreaking—but it’s not unfamiliar.

The enemy is real. “The thief comes only to steal and kill and destroy; I have come that they may have life, and have it to the full” (John 10:10). This is our burden—to reroute destinies like Gustavo’s and help young people find life, purpose, and hope through relationships that point them to Jesus Christ and the comprehensive plan He has for them.

We believe that, together, we can be a symbol of life and light—like the Freedom Tower standing tall in Lower Manhattan—a beacon of resilience, redemption, and rising anew. That is the vision for Young Life Greater NYC.

We invite you to walk even deeper with us: share this mission, pray fervently, and give generously. Your investment isn't just changing lives—it's saving them. With deep gratitude and hope,
